About Me

I was born in the Dominican Republic. I grew up in the city of Santo Domingo. In 2012, I moved to State College, PA. Where I got my Bachelor's degree in Engineering from The Pennsylvania State University, Penn State. At the end of 2015, when I finished my bachelor's degree, I moved back to the Dominican Republic and worked in Telecommunications for around 1 year. In 2017, I moved to Vancouver, Canada to do a Master's degree in Business Administration from University Canada West. Completed my MBA degree from UCW in March, 2019. From 2018 to 2019, I worked in Telecommunications companies in Canada. In April 2019, I moved back to the Dominican Republic. Then, in December 2021, I moved back to Vancouver. Since the beginning of 2022 until now, I've worked with different temp agencies and mobile gig apps, in different roles, doing daily and weekly gigs/shifts.
